Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Whitewater Rafting site is to provide an online presence for our rafting business, showcasing our services, trips, and safety measures to attract adventure enthusiasts.

Audience

The target audience for the rafting website includes adventure seekers, families looking for outdoor activities, and corporate groups planning team-building events. Scenario questions might include:

  • What safety measures are in place for first-time rafters?
  • Are there family-friendly rafting options?
  • Can we book corporate group events online?
